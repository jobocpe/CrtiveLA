About

Crtive.LA is a student-produced podcast that explores the lives and work of people across a wide range of creative fields in Los Angeles. Each episode is recorded live with an audience at 4:00 PM on the first Tuesday of the month in the school’s photo studio. Get tickets here!

The podcast was conceived in 2025 by Oliver Shipley, a photography teacher at Ramon C. Corine School of Visual & Performing Arts, with the goal of giving students hands-on experience in media production while learning directly from the creative professionals. Crtive.LA brings together storytelling, education, and community—offering a unique platform for both students and local creatives to share their journeys.
