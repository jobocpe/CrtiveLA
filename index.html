ABOUT

Crtive.LA is a student-produced podcast that explores the lives and work of people across a wide range of creative fields in Los Angeles. Each episode is recorded live with an audience at 4:00 PM on the first Thursday of the month at Ramon C. Cortines School of Visual & Performing Arts located at the iconic Coop Himmelblau building in Downtown Los Angeles.

The project’s goal is to give students hands-on experience in media production while learning directly from creative professionals. Crtive.LA brings together storytelling, education, and community—offering a unique platform for both students and local creatives to share and learn.

The podcast was launched in 2025 by Oliver Shipley, a photography teacher at the school.
